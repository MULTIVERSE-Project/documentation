mvp.Button

Alternative to default DButton.

When activated, the "mvp.Button" panel is design to display text or an icon indicating its function. The panel also have additional customizable features, such as the ability to change its color or size. Additionally, the panel may be programmed to respond to different types of user interactions, such as a single click or a double-click, depending on the requirements of the script.
